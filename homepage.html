Cat Biology

The Science
Behind Cats

Cats are small, carnivorous mammals from the Felidae family, known for their agility, sharp claws, and retractable claws. Domesticated for nearly 10,000 years, cats share 95.6% of their genetic makeup with tigers. They have excellent night vision, which helps them hunt in low light, and their whiskers are highly sensitive, helping them navigate even the narrowest spaces.

Cat Behavior

Understanding Cat Behavior

Ever wondered why your cat kneads or purrs? Cats communicate through body language, vocalizations, and scent marking. Playful by nature, they also have strong hunting instincts, and their independence has made them popular companions for busy lifestyles.

Cat Breeds

Popular Cat Breeds

Siamese
Siamese cats are sleek and elegant, with short fur and striking blue almond-shaped eyes. They typically have light-colored bodies with darker points on their ears, face, paws, and tail. Known for their vocal and social nature, Siamese cats are extremely affectionate and enjoy interacting with humans. They thrive in environments where they receive lots of attention, and daily playtime is essential to keep them mentally stimulated.
Persian
Persian cats are known for their flat faces, luxurious long fur, and round eyes. They have a calm and laid-back personality, preferring a quiet, stable environment. Persians are affectionate but not overly energetic, making them excellent indoor pets. Their long fur requires daily brushing to keep it tangle-free, and regular eye cleaning is often necessary. Persians enjoy lounging and prefer a peaceful atmosphere.
Puspin
Puspins (short for "Pusang Pinoy") are mixed-breed domestic cats native to the Philippines. They come in various colors, patterns, and sizes, making each one unique. Known for their resilience and adaptability, Puspins are often found as street cats but also make wonderful pets. They are generally friendly, affectionate, and independent, with a playful yet relaxed nature. Puspins require minimal grooming and are relatively low-maintenance, making them ideal for busy households. They are highly adaptable to different living conditions, whether indoors or outdoors.
